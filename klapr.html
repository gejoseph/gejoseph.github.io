Klapr

Internship / UI/UX Design

DURATION: May - August 2021

PLATFORM: iOS

STATUS: Development Stage

This past summer I worked as a freelance UI/UX designer for a small startup called Klapr through a part-time internship with Caravel Associates. Klapr is a mobile app that connects street performers with spectators. Klapr allows anyone to become a patron of the arts, making it quick and easy for spectators to discover buskers and street performances, and for performers to gain popularity and get tips with a quick click.

Methods

Wireframing

Prototyping

Tools

Adobe XD

Adobe Photoshop

I was given a set of existing wireframes and asked to improve the user experience. My overhaul of the wireframes primarily focuses on creating a more cohesive experience that builds off of existing mental models produced by mass familiarity with more ubiquitous user interfaces. For example, Klapr includes a search similar to that of Google Maps, but specific to street performers. I drew from the layout of Google Maps in including toggles and navigation displays in the search nearby user flow. Additionally, most of the original choices in the font, font-sizing, line-spacing, component alignment, and information architecture needed work so I made adjustments in those areas and informed developers of their importance.

This experience ultimately allowed me to become well-versed with Adobe XD, a tool that I was unfamiliar with initially. Additionally, I learned how to work with existing wireframes and how to conduct a design review. Prior to this internship, my primary UI/UX experience concerned creating wireframes/prototypes from scratch, so this opportunity enabled me to understand what it's like to come in at a later stage and focus more on iteration.
